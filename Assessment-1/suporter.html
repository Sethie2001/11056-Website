Thank you for your support!
Hello and thank you all contour 556 supporters. We closed our first fundraising campaign through chuffed on 26th June 2021. We received $9150 through the platform and a further $6600 directly.
That is a great start for the 2022 event and it is wonderful to have support from such a wide group of people, some of whom we haven't met yet! Your donation makes art possible. If you missed this out there is still time for you to donate through our website. More funds = more artists. Thanks again and we look forward to seeing you all in Canberra next year!
